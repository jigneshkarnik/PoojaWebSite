Community Engagement

In addition to my clinical work, I am actively involved in the community through social service initiatives. I volunteer with organizations - Stichting Sanskriti, Sanskar, and Uithoorn Voor Elkaar, where I contribute to health-focused events and outreach. Through regular involvement in Dutch working groups for classical homeopathy, I share insights from my Indian background and experience, contributing to the wider professional community.

My patients often commend my thorough, understanding, and compassionate approach, with elderly patients in particular appreciating my empathy and patience.

Women's Health and Hormonal Problems

In today's fast-paced world, where women juggle responsibilities across family, work, and social life, it's easy to overlook the importance of hormonal health. Yet, hormonal balance is crucial for overall well-being.

I led an insightful session in #Uithoorn, organized by Uithoorn voor Elkaar and Stichting Sanskriti Aur Sanskar, where I highlighted the often-overlooked connection between hormonal health and women's daily lives. The event, held at Vrouwen Middag, brought together women from diverse backgrounds for an interactive and informative discussion.

India Day, Uithoorn

On 23rd November 2024, Stichting Sanskriti Aur Sanskar and Amstelring Het Hoge Heem came together to celebrate a truly memorable India Day at Het Hoge Heem, Uithoorn.

Being part of both organizations provided me the unique privilege to bridge Indian and Dutch cultures, bringing people, traditions, and cuisine together in harmony. While visiting Het Hoge Heem is always a fulfilling experience, India Day was truly extraordinary.

Volunteer at Het Hoge Heem

I volunteer weekly at Het Hoge Heem, an old age home in Uithoorn, where I assist elderly residents in spending meaningful, fulfilling days. Providing my services at this facility is always a satisfying and fulfilling experience.

Through regular engagement with the residents, I've developed deep connections and understand the importance of compassionate care for our elderly. Every visit brings new opportunities to make a positive difference in their lives.

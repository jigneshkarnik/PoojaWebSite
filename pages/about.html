About Dr Pooja Karnik

Dr Pooja Karnik is a registered classical homeopath (BHMS) with extensive experience in treating a wide range of acute and chronic conditions. Based in Uithoorn, Netherlands, she offers compassionate, evidence-informed homeopathic care to individuals and families.

Professional Background

With a Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine & Surgery (B.H.M.S) degree, Dr. Karnik has dedicated her practice to the principles of classical homeopathy as established by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann. She continues to advance her knowledge through regular professional development and training programs, including specialized courses in biochemic tissue salts, pediatric homeopathy, and constitutional prescribing.

Practice Philosophy

Dr. Karnik believes in treating the whole person—body, mind, and spirit. Her approach goes beyond managing symptoms to address the underlying causes of illness. Each patient receives individualized attention through comprehensive case-taking that considers physical complaints, emotional states, lifestyle factors, and personal history.

She is committed to creating a safe, non-judgmental space where patients feel comfortable sharing their health concerns and can actively participate in their healing journey.

It means substances which can cause symptoms in a healthy individual can cure similar symptoms in a diseased individual.

Homeopathy is an individualistically oriented science. No two patients get the same remedy even though they are suffering from the same disease.

Homeopathic medicines are Natural, Safe and free from any side effects. They stimulate the body's natural defence mechanism and accelerate the healing process.

Can I take homeopathy medicines while on allopathy treatment?

In today's world when the patient is already taking many allopathic medicines for diabetes, blood pressure, heart problems; homeopathy and allopathy should/can work hand in hand. The aim of such treatment is to gradually get rid of the need to take medicine, step by step.

The dosage is so small, how can it work?

It is the same as asking how can a small atom cause an atomic blast, or how can a small spark cause a wildfire. The human body is capable of fighting diseases on its own. What is needed is the right trigger for it to work. The skill of the homeopathy practitioner is to provide the correct spark, in correct intensities and in correct dosage.

Sometimes I am advised not to take medicine for weeks and still get cured, how?

The human body is a highly sophisticated machine. Even though it is capable of healing itself, it needs time. No one likes constant hammering, even our body. Frequently administering medicines all the time can interfere in the healing process and sometimes can even stop it. The practitioner decides, case by case basis and based upon progress, when and how much medicine to give next, and that's where experience comes handy.

Homeopathy is based on the principle of "Similia Similibus Curentur" which means "Like cures Like." Homeopathy is an individualistically oriented science. No two patients get the same remedy even though they are suffering from the same disease.

Are homeopathy and herbal medicine the same?

No, Homeopathy itself is an independent holistic medicinal system. While some homeopathic medicines are prepared from plants, homeopathy is not a branch of any other system of medicine.

Are all homeopathic medicines the same white pills?

The white pills are only the medium for dispensing medicine to the patient. The liquid is actually the medicine. The tiny white globules don't have any medicinal property themselves.

This is a common myth, but it is not always true. It differs from patient to patient.
